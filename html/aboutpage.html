About Us






Foxy Pizza, where passion meets flavor! Since 2008.


At Foxy Pizza, we believe in crafting every pizza with the freshest ingredients to satisfy every taste. But we're more than just great pizza. At Foxy Pizza, we pride ourselves on excellent customer service and a cozy, friendly atmosphere. and every bite leaves you wanting more.

Foxy Pizza was founded in 2008 with a mission to bring exceptional pizza to our community. Starting as a small, family-owned pizzeria, we quickly became known for our delicious, hand-crafted pizzas made with the freshest ingredients. Today, we continue to uphold our founding principles, and friendly atmosphere that our customers have loved from the beginning.
